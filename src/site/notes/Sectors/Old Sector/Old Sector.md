**Overview**  
The [[Sectors/Old Sector/Old Sector\|Old Sector]] is a territory controlled by humans where Digimon laws do not apply. The landscape is largely scarred by the wars of the past, with the majority of the human population residing within the safety of [[Sectors/Old Sector/Amaterasu/Amaterasu City\|Amaterasu City]]. This region stands as a symbol of human resilience, but also a constant reminder of what was lost.

**Economy & Operations**  
Humanity maintains its presence and economic stability by dispatching protected trade caravans from the [[Sectors/Old Sector/Old Sector\|Old Sector]] to established safe zones in other sectors. These caravans, often run by the [[Organizations/Freelancer/Freelancer Guild\|Freelancer Guild]] or independent human companies, are the lifelines of the sector.

**Governance**  
All human activity within this territory is governed by a strict legal framework known as the [[13 Rules of the Old Sector\|13 Rules of the Old Sector]].
