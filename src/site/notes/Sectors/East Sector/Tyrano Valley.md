Deep in "[[Sectors/East Sector/East Sector\|East Sector]]" there's a canyon full of dinosaur bones. Its called "Tyrano Valley" this place is a home for many big dinosaur like Digimon, known as a dangerous and wild place. However, on top of the highest mountain inside the valley there's a resting place of the old MasterTyranomon who once ruled this place till he got destroyed by Gru the MetalGarurumon. Next to this resting place there's "[[Sectors/East Sector/Tyro Village\|Tyro Village]]" where many Digimon live in more civilized manner but still in quite primitive life conditions.

A deep and dangerous canyon, famously filled with the ancient bones of prehistoric creatures. This wild region is home to many large, dinosaur-like Digimon and is a perilous place for unguided travelers. On top of the highest mountain within the valley lies the resting place of [[MasterTyranomon\|MasterTyranomon]], who once ruled here until his defeat by [[People/East/Gru\|Gru]] the [[MetalGarurumon\|MetalGarurumon]].
