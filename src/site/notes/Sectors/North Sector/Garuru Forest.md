The largest forest in the [[Sectors/North Sector/North Sector\|North Sector]], stretching for days. It is rich with animals and forageable food. Icy lakes hide abundant fish beneath their surfaces. The forest is beautiful, silent, and sparsely populated, mostly by hunters. It covers nearly one-third of the sector and is home to rapidly multiplying animals from the Human world, providing a sustainable source of food.
