The [[Sectors/North Sector/North Sector\|North Sector]] is known for its extremely cold and unwelcoming environment, characterized by dangerous weather with frequent blizzards that block roads. The climate is perpetually cold and snowy. Despite the harsh conditions, the local populace is notably friendly to everyone. The region is excellent for mineral collection and hunting, and its stark landscape possesses a severe beauty. The night sky is consistently clear, filled with stars and the aurora borealis (northern lights).

Governance & Security
The [[Sectors/North Sector/North Sector\|North Sector]] is highly united under a single main force known as the [[Organizations/Rapid Response Force\|Organizations/Rapid Response Force]] (RRF).


Additionally, the [[Organizations/Freelancer/Freelancer Guild\|Freelancer Guild]] operates efficiently in the [[Sectors/North Sector/North Sector\|North Sector]], handling various tasks for payment. The guild is well-organized and receives financial support from the local ruler to assist with tasks that do not require the RRF's immediate attention.
