**Overview**  
The [[Sectors/North Sector/North Sector\|North Sector]] is a land defined by its extreme cold and often harsh, unwelcoming environment, where frequent blizzards can swiftly block roads and the climate remains perpetually snowy. Despite these formidable conditions, its local populace is renowned for its universal friendliness. The region is rich in mineral deposits, offering excellent opportunities for collection, and its vast wilderness supports abundant hunting. Beyond its practical resources, the [[Sectors/North Sector/North Sector\|North Sector]] possesses a severe and stark beauty, particularly evident at night, when the sky is consistently clear, filled with countless stars and the mesmerizing dance of the aurora borealis.

**Governance & Security**  
The [[Sectors/North Sector/North Sector\|North Sector]] is uniquely united under a single, formidable main force known as the [[Organizations/Rapid Response Force\|Rapid Response Force]]. In times of peace, the RRF operates as the sector's police and rescue service, venturing beyond [[Sectors/North Sector/Genbu City\|Genbu City]] to assist in disasters or emergency calls. During more serious conflicts, it functions as a full-fledged army. The RRF is highly organized, meticulously trained, and equipped with specialized gear for arctic travel. All members undergo extensive training in first aid before being accepted. Though smaller than other sector armies due to its rigorous standards, the RRF is prepared for any contingency. It is an integrated force, proudly accepting both Digimon and Humans into its ranks, who operate in mixed squads.

Additionally, the [[Organizations/Freelancer/Freelancer Guild\|Freelancer Guild]] maintains a strong and efficient presence in the [[Sectors/North Sector/North Sector\|North Sector]]. They undertake various tasks for payment and are well-organized, receiving financial support from [[People/North/Genbu/Blazer\|Blazer]], the local ruler, to handle missions that do not require the RRF's immediate attention.

**Food Served in the North Sector**  
The culinary offerings of the [[Sectors/North Sector/North Sector\|North Sector]] are robust and comforting, tailored to its frigid environment:

- **Hot Beverages:** Tea, Blue Tea, Hot Chocolate.
- **Cold Beverages:** Slushies, Vanilla Shake, Chocolate Shake, Yak Milk, Goat Milk, Spring Water, Sparkling Water, Beer, Mera Beer, Frost Wine, Frost Berry Juice.
- **Meats:** Yak Meat, Yak Steak, Yak Kebab, Deer Meat, Deer Steak, Deer Kebab, Rabbit Meat, Roasted Rabbit, Rabbit Kebab.
- **Hearty Meals:** Hamburger, Burger, Cheeseburger, Pizza, Spaghetti, Eggs, Scrambled Eggs, Boiled Eggs.
- **Fruits & Desserts:** Frosty Berries, Dried Frosty Berries, Pinecone Pie, Fish, Vanilla Ice Cream, Chocolate Ice Cream.
