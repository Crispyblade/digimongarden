Every [[Organizations/Freelancer/Guild House\|Guild House]] across the Digital World shares a similar adventurous theme and functional layout. They serve as central hubs for [[Organizations/Freelancer/Freelancer Guild\|Freelancer Guild]] activity within each city.

**Standard Facilities Include:**

- **Bounty Boards:** Multiple boards constantly updated with available quests and missions.
- **Registration Desks:** For registering new teams, updating member status, and filing mission reports.
- **Guild Shops:** Offering unique Guild-exclusive items and equipment.
- **Repair Shop:** A small facility for members to repair their gear or craft their own items.
- **Basic Clinic:** Providing essential medical assistance to members.
- **Food Stall & Vending Machines:** Offering sustenance and refreshments.
- **Hall of Fame:** Walls adorned with portraits of registered teams, highlighting active and famous members.
- **Local Guild Master Office:** The operational center for the Guild's local leadership.
