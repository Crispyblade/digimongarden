**Overview**  
The [[Organizations/Freelancer/Freelancer Guild\|Freelancer Guild]] is a powerful and influential neutral organization, serving as a contractor for bounties and local missions across the Digital World. It maintains significant influence among both the [[Organizations/Digimon Council\|Digimon Council]] and the [[Sectors/Old Sector/Phoenix Kingdom\|Phoenix Kingdom]], acting as a crucial intermediary between these often-conflicting powers. The Guild offers well-paying missions and provides a range of services for its members, all dependent on their official rank.

**Core Values & Mission**  
As envoys of both humanity and Digimon, all Guild members are required to prioritize diplomacy and attempt peaceful resolutions before resorting to violence. The Guild aims to maintain neutrality and stability, striving to prevent larger conflicts while providing essential services and opportunities for its diverse members.

### **[[Guild Rules\|Guild Rules]] & Conduct**

**Team Registration Rules:**

1. Each team must be officially registered with the Guild.
2. Every team requires a unique name, logo, and motto.
3. Teams must have a minimum of three members and a maximum of ten members.
4. Each team must designate a Captain and a Second-in-Command.
5. Higher ranks unlock greater rewards, a broader selection of missions, and more challenging tasks.
6. Every team is required to include at least one Digimon member.
7. All human members within a team must be registered.
8. The Guild offers unique items for sale exclusively to higher-ranked teams.
9. All teams operate independently, but the Guild strictly prohibits banditry or criminal activities.
