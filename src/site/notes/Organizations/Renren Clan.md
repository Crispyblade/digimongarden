### **Renren Clan**

The [[Organizations/Renren Clan\|Renren Clan]] is an exclusive clan composed primarily of Renamon, who are sworn protectors of the [[Sectors/South Sector/Suzaku Temple\|Suzaku Temple]] and its vital Digi-Egg rebirth zone in the [[Sectors/South Sector/South Sector\|South Sector]]. They maintain trade relations with the local [[Sectors/South Sector/Florapalm Village\|Florapalm Village]], exchanging food for supplies. The clan is led by the wise and seasoned [[People/South/Renren/Furella\|Furella]], with the formidable [[People/South/Renren/Versaka\|Versaka]] serving as its primary protector.

The [[Organizations/Renren Clan\|Renren Clan]] sustains itself through traditional methods: hunting in the surrounding jungle, gathering fruits and slap berries from the nearby [[Sectors/South Sector/Kuwagamon Grove\|Kuwagamon Grove]], rice farming, and fishing. Clan members are treated as lifelong family, fostering deep bonds. Their core focus lies in the rigorous training of martial arts and magic, while their economy thrives on food production and the trade of ancient knowledge. The clan also proudly claims [[Sectors/South Sector/Kirvera Mountain\|Kirvera Mountain]] as part of their ancestral heritage.

**Renren Clan Ranks**

- **Little Fox:** The lowest rank for beginners, typically not permitted to leave the temple alone.
- **Young Kitsune:** Members who have completed basic teachings and can operate without direct supervision.
- **Adept Kitsune:** Skilled members nearing the completion of their studies under a teacher.
- **Guardian Kitsune:** Warriors, hunters, and guards focused on combat and physical disciplines.
- **Scholar Kitsune:** Members dedicated to wisdom, magic, traditions, and everyday skills, emphasizing enlightenment and peaceful solutions.
- **Grand Kitsune:** An elite rank given to the clan's second-in-command or a select few who have mastered all skills.
- **Clan Leader:** The highest rank, whose word is absolute. The leader is the most wise and experienced, responsible for the clan's future and training a successor.

**Renren Clan Customs**

- **Communal Sleeping:** Renamon often sleep together in piles for warmth and protection, with one always keeping watch during their shift.
- **Meditation:** A sacred practice mandatory at least once a week to train spirit and will.
- **Diet:** Clan members typically consume rice, cooked meat, slap berries, tofu, fruits, and fish-focused dishes. They drink water, juice, and on rare occasions, sake.
- **Slap Berry Collection:** This task is considered both a chore and a form of training, as unripe berries will "slap" the hands of those who try to pick them.
- **Student Obedience:** Students must follow the orders of their teachers and masters, regardless of how challenging or unusual the training may seem.
- **Communal Responsibility:** Every clan member, regardless of rank, contributes to the daily upkeep and operation of the temple.
- **Temple Defense:** Every clan member is expected to defend the temple if required, even if it is not a formal rule.
- **Massage as Bonding:** Offering a deep massage is considered a declaration of friendship or a profound bonding experience.

**Renren Clan Rules**

1. Always listen to the Leader of the clan.
2. The name given by a teacher to a student becomes their recognized second name.
3. Clan members receive a permanent tattoo of the [[Organizations/Renren Clan\|Renren Clan]] mark on their left shoulder.
4. Death battles between clan members are strictly forbidden.
5. A student's master has the right to punish them according to their own judgment.
6. Protect your clan members and the temple at all costs.
7. The clan is sworn to protect Digi-Eggs from harm.
