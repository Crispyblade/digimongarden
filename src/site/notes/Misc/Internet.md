In the Digital World, there are not multiple internets, but **two interconnected layers** of a single, global network. Think of it as a city (the Surface Net) and the vast, unmapped sewer and tunnel system running beneath it (the Data Underbelly). How you experience the city depends entirely on which district you're in.

The Digital Net was a post-war collaboration between human engineers and cooperative Digimon, primarily Data-types like Andromon and Guardromon. The goal was to create a stable communication network to facilitate governance, trade, and cultural exchange under the new Digimon Council's authority.

Unlike the human world's physical infrastructure of fiber optic cables, the Digital Net operates on a principle of "Signal Resonance" through "Digital Waves", which are random and unreliable. Massive broadcast towers, often integrated into key structures like the Grand Windmill in Syriou or the Council Spire in Asuka, emit a constant, stable "carrier wave" of benign data. Datapads and other devices "resonate" with this wave, piggybacking their own signals onto it to communicate. This makes the Net strongest in major cities and along key trade routes where "Signal Boosters" are maintained. Venture too far into the wilds, the wastelands, or deep underground, and the signal fades into digital static, leaving you completely isolated.

### **Layer 1: The Digital Net (The Surface Net)**

This is the common, public-facing internet used for everyday communication, commerce, and entertainment.

**Core Technology: Signal Resonance**  
The Net doesn't rely on physical cables. Instead, massive towers in major cities emit a stable "carrier wave." Devices like datapads "resonate" with this wave to send and receive data. This makes the connection strongest in cities and along major routes with signal boosters. In the wilderness or remote areas, the signal fades to nothing, enforcing isolation.

**The Experience of the Surface Net varies drastically by region:**

#### **A) In Digimon Council Controlled Sectors (Asuka, Syriou, etc.)**

- **Theme:** The Gilded Cage
- **Method of Control:** **Censorship and Propaganda.** The Council's primary goal is stability and the preservation of their authority. Their Net is a tool to enforce this.
- **User Experience:**
    - **Heavily Sanitized:** The network is patrolled by powerful censor programs. Any content deemed seditious, anti-Council, or disruptive is simply deleted. Searches for sensitive topics (like the BPO, human rights abuses, or Council corruption) will return no results or lead to heavily biased, official articles.
    - **Propaganda is News:** The default homepage on all standard datapads is the "Council Daily," which features stories that paint the Council and its allies in a positive light. It's a constant stream of information designed to create a sense of security and obedience.
    - **Purpose:** To keep the populace docile, distracted, and compliant. It is safe, clean, and devoid of challenging ideas.

#### **B) In Amaterasu City (The Human Kingdom)**

- **Theme:** The Public Square
- **Method of Control:** **Protection and Transparency.** Queen Sabrina Flamewing believes an informed citizen is a strong citizen. Her goal is not to control thought, but to defend her people from digital threats and disinformation.
- **User Experience:**
    - **The Royal Shield:** Amaterasu does not have a censorship firewall. It has a defensive "Digital Shield." This system does not delete information. Instead, it:
        1. **Neutralizes Threats:** Actively destroys viruses, malware, and direct hacking attempts. Its goal is to protect users and infrastructure, not police ideas.
        2. **Tags Disinformation:** It identifies content from known propaganda sources (like the BPO) and applies a system-generated warning label, such as `[Source Unverified]` or `[Warning: This content originates from a hostile political organization.]`. This allows citizens to see the information but make their own informed decision.
    - **Radical Transparency:** Sabrina's government uses the Net to post public records, meeting notes, and threat assessments. They fight propaganda by providing a constant stream of verifiable, truthful information.
    - **Purpose:** To create a resilient, intelligent populace capable of discerning truth from fiction. The Amaterasu Net is a vibrant, chaotic, and often argumentative place, but it is fundamentally free.

### **Layer 2: The Data Underbelly (The Shadow Net)**

The Underbelly is the single, worldwide "dark web." It is not a separate network but a parasitic, encrypted layer hidden within the cracks of the Surface Net. It is a lawless, dangerous, and anonymous space accessible from anywhere, provided you have the right software and skills.

**Universal Features of the Underbelly:**

- **The Inhabitants:** It is a haven for all who operate outside the law. Information brokers, criminals, assassins, data-thieves, slavers, and revolutionaries all conduct their business here.
- **The Dangers:** The Underbelly is rife with predatory programs, data wraiths, and aggressive viruses that can steal funds, corrupt data, or track a user's physical location. Every interaction carries risk.
- **The Economy:** It is a thriving black market where anything can be bought or sold, from illegal weapons and slave contracts to state secrets and corporate espionage.

**The Underbelly as a Political Battleground:**

While the Underbelly is global, the conflict is most intense in the digital space surrounding Amaterasu City. This is the epicenter of the human-BPO cold war.

- **The Black Phoenix Organization (The Aggressors):** The BPO are the masters of the Underbelly. They run the most prominent forums and "uncensored" news sites. Their goals are to spread their radical propaganda, recruit disillusioned humans, and gather intelligence by data-mining anyone who visits their sites. They are the loud, dominant, and aggressive force.
- **The Queen's Ghosts (The Defenders):** Queen Sabrina's intelligence agents operate silently in the Underbelly. They are not there to censor or destroy. Their mission is defensive:
    - They perform **counter-intelligence**, identifying credible threats of violence or terrorism from the BPO and relaying them to the Royal Guard.
    - They **de-escalate**, subtly intervening to protect vulnerable citizens from BPO radicalization.
    - They **fight lies with truth**, anonymously leaking verifiable documents and evidence to counteract BPO propaganda, allowing the Underbelly's own ecosystem to debunk the falsehoods.
